Mushroom Biryani

A simple biryani recipe for the family!

Mushroom biryani is a dish popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, herbs, flavorful spices and mushroom. This is slow cooked for a richer flavor, so make sure you have the time!

Ingredients (mushroom marinade)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt (dahi)
  • 1 1/2 tsps minced ginger (adrak)
  • 1 1/2 tsps minced garlic (lahsun)
  • 1/2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin (jeera)>
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander (dhaniya)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (namak)
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric (haldi)>
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder (laal mirch powder)
  • 4 oz mushroom
    • Ingredients (biryani)
    • 8 to 10 saffron thread (kesar)
    • 1 tbsp milk (doodh)
      • <1i>1 cup basmati rice (basmati chaaval) <1i>2 tbsp ghee <1i>1/4 cup canola oil <1i>4 oz assorted mushroom (marinade) <1i>1 large onion thinly sliced (pyaaj)
    • salt (namak)
    • 2 cardamom pods (ilaichi)
      • <1i>2 cloves (laung) <1i>1/2 fresh and finely minced cilantro/mint (dhaniya/pudina)
Steps
  1. Prepare marinade by combining marinade ingredients listed in a medium bowl and marinate for 30 mins at room temperature.
  2. Seperately, prepare saffron milk by crushing the saffron threads with mortar pestle to form a powder. Mix it with milk.
  3. For the rice, rinse in sieve the rice and clean the grains. Soak it for at least 30 mins.
  4. Par-boil large pot with 8 cups water and add rice once boiled. Cook for 5 or 6 minutes. Drain rice and take 1/2 cup of rice that is tossed with ghee. Put it aside.
  5. Fry mushrooms and onions with canola oil with medium-heat. Fry for 8 to 10 minutes. Put it out and salt it.
  6. Cook mushrrom gravy with residual oil and medium-heat. Add the cardamom and cloves until sizzling. Saute for 6 to 7 minutes and add the reserved rice sock. Add the mushroom and onions as well until gravy is thick.
  7. Add half of rice and spread evenly in dutch oven. Add the mushroom gravy and half the cilantro. Use the remaining ghee across top and layer the other half of rice. Pour the saffron milk over with the fried mushroom/onion and cilantro. Cover.
  8. For cooking, set to medium heat and cook for 5 mins. Reduce heat to low and cook for 20 minutes. Then turn the heat off and leave for 10 minutes.
