Pav Bhaji

A simple pav bhaji recipe for the party!

Mushroom biryani is a popular steet food originating in Mumbai, India. It consists of a thick mashed vegetable curry (Bhaji) and soft bread buns. (Pav)

Ingredients (Bhaji))
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 finely chopped tomatoes (tamatar)
  • 1/4 cup peas (matar)
  • 1/2 capsicum finely chopped (shimla mirch)
  • 2 boiled + mashed potato (aloo)>
  • 1 tsp salt (dhaniya)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (namak)
  • 1 1/4 tsp kasmiri red chili powder (lal mirch powder)>
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric (haldi)
  • 1 1/2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 2 tsp dry fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • 3 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp finely chopped onion (pyaaj)
  • 1/2 lemon juice (nimbu paani)
  • water for consistency
Ingredients (Toast Pav)
  • 8 bread buns(pav)
  • 4 tsp butter
    • <1i>1/2 tsp kashmiru red chilli powder <1i>1/2 tsp pav bjahi masala <1i>4 tsp finely chopped coriander leaves
Steps
  1. In a large kadai heat butter and vegetables, cook+mash
  2. Add chilli powder, turmeric, pav bhaji masala, kasuri methi and coriander leaves
  3. Heat all the Toast Pav ingredients
  4. Boil and mash for 5 minutes.
